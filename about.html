The MSUC was founded in 2020 by a small, multidisciplinary team of clinicians (registered psychologists, nurse practitioners, etc.), who have been supporting people with substance dependence over the years.

OUR APPROACH

Our main approach used during treatment is clinical hypnosis, which is the underpinning psychological approach for all our outpatient treatment programs. (Clinical hypnotherapy is an evidence-based treatment for addiction, as well as a range of other psychological difficulties and mental health conditions).

At the MSUC we provide treatment to:

  • Ease the withdrawal symptoms
  • Manage substance-related cravings
  • Support you to maintain your abstinence from your substance(s) of concern.

As substance use disorders commonly occur with other mental illnesses (such as depression or anxiety), we also provide treatment for the co-occurring mental health conditions.

    Examples of this include:
  • A mental health problem or disorder leading to or associated with problematic alcohol and/or other drug use
  • A substance use disorder leading to or associated with mental health issues
  • Alcohol and/or other drug misuse worsening or altering the course of a person’s mental illness.

The MSUC aims to provide its clients with the best care possible. Our clinicians are highly qualified, extensively trained in clinical hypnosis and passionate about supporting people whose goal is to put an end to their substance dependence.

We believe that every symptom (including substance dependence) is the creative (psychological, mental and/or behavioural) “product” of the individual. As such, no one has the right to label them as “wrong”, something that needs to be fixed or corrected, or take them away from the person whom they belong to. What we can do though is to walk beside you during your self-discovery and self-development, so that you don’t have to do it on your own.
