DOWNLOADING ZOOM

  1. Go to zoom.us/DOWNLOAD
  2. Select “Zoom Client for meetings” and download to your prefered device
  3. Install the “ZoomInstaller” application onto your device
  4. The “Zoom Cloudmeetings” icon should appear on your screen.

USING ZOOM

  1. You will receive an appointment confirmation email with the meeting URL after booking
  2. Please click this URL to start the meeting, or you can “Sign-in” then select “Join” and enter meeting ID from the email received
  3. Ensure your device has microphone and webcam.

DOWNLOADING SKYPE

  1. Visit the following link >>
  2. Select “Get skype” to your prefered device
  3. Install “Skype” application onto your computer
  4. Create a Skype account then “Log-in”.

USING SKYPE

  1. You will receive an appointment confirmation email with the meeting date and time and your clinician's skype name
  2. After “Log-in”, click on the search bar at the top left hand corner of the Skype screen and enter the skype name of your clinician into the search bar
  3. Click on your clinician's profile
  4. To start the meeting, click on the video link button – the video camera icon near the top right hand side of the screen.
