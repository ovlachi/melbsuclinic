PRIVATE OUTPATIENT TREATMENT PROGRAMS

These programs have been specifically designed to provide the most effective chance for your success and recovery. With limited spaces in these individual programs, please contact us now for an initial appointment to secure your place.

"Strong Mind"

    The goal of this program is: “Staying determined!” This 20 week outpatient program aims to support you in:
  • Gradually building inner strength and confidence, so that you don’t have to rely on substance use as a coping mechanism
  • Learning hypno-relaxation as a way to manage withdrawal symptoms, anxiety, stress, and experience deep relaxation and calmness in a natural way
  • Developing strategies to handle cravings and urges through imagery conditioning, etc.

PRICE: $4,600

Book now

"Resilient Soul"

    The motto of this program is: “You have the power to change YOUR world”. This 30-week outpatient program aims to support you in:
  • Gradually building inner strength and confidence, so that you don’t have to rely on substance use as a coping mechanism
  • Learning hypno-relaxation as a way to manage withdrawal symptoms, anxiety, stress, and experience deep relaxation and calmness in a natural way
  • Working on the deeper levels of your personality so that you can access and work on healing the root causes of your current difficulties and struggles in life – one of which may be your alcohol/drug dependence.

PRICE: $6,900
